About Us

ED-Cell, JUET GUNA aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the ‘Ideas’ in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.

Events

What We Do.

E-Cell organises a variety of events throughout the year to promote entrepreneurial activities in and around the campus. Our annual E-Summit witnesses huge participation from all over India. Events like Shark Tank and IPL Auction take the learning to a whole new level.

Shark Tank

Shark Tank JUET is an entrepreneurial event held at JUET College, where participants pitch their innovative business ideas to a panel of judges, often faculty members or experienced entrepreneurs. Similar to the popular TV show "Shark Tank," contestants present their concepts, business plans, and prototypes to seek potential investment and guidance.

IPL Auction

The IPL Auction JUET is an exciting event organized at JUET College, inspired by the Indian Premier League (IPL) cricket tournament. In this unique college-level competition, teams of five participants act as virtual IPL team owners. They are given a fixed budget, much like real IPL franchises, and their goal is to strategically bid for the best cricket players available for selection.
